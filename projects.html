Diablo III AutoClick


I usually play games to help blow off some steam as well as try out some features these games have. For Diablo III, there are a lot of classes that require players to smash your keyboard constantly. Therefore, I rebuilt this support application in 2019 from an online source for myself in case my hands hurt. It was easy to use and fairly straightforward with a clean and simple design.

Scott Platon Online Apparel Shop


Established in the beginning of 2020 and officially launched in August, Scott Platon is one of the most current projects I am working on. The business created a new fashion trend in Vietnam: minimalist clothes with high quality and affordable prices. It also gained more than 30,000 followers on Instagram upon creating an account there. I am the co-founder and currently managing the website while finding new ideas for our business.
